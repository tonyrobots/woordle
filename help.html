How to Play

Welcome to Worderly! The goal is to guess the hidden word.

When you guess a word, you receive feedback on each letter. It's like Wordle, but with a twist -- the clues relate to where each letter in the answer falls alphabetically in relation to your guess.

  • A: Correct letter in the correct position.
  • B: The correct letter for this position comes after your guess in the alphabet.
  • Z: The correct letter for this position comes before your guess in the alphabet.

Use this feedback to narrow down the possibilities and guess the word!
